What is RCRD STOR?

RCRD STOR is a new and upcoming social network all about discussing, listening to, recommending, and discovering music.

With RCRD STOR you can catalogue any album you have listened to, along with a rating and short review. You can view the catalogue and reveiws of other users and recommend each other new music. Build your circle of music reviewers today

What can you do with RCRD STOR?

Follow

Follow and keep updated on your favorite artists. Get notified when they release new music or merchandise, or whenever they hold a concert in your area. New releases open up new discussion posts for you to talk about that band's new release with their other followers.

Review

After listening to an album, you have the option to leave a 1 to 10 rating score on the album, along with an optional short description behind the rating. You can view ratings other users have made for similar albums, or share and see the reviews of all your friends. These reviews can be private or public.

Recommend

On your profile, you can post a list of characteristics you are looking for in a song or album, and have other community members recommend you something new to listen to. They can't recommend you something that's already on your "listened to" list, so don't worry about getting repeats

Catalogue

Keep track of each album you have listened to, along with your rating. You and friends can share lists and narrow the list down to similarities or differences between the two.
